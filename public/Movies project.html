The Best Movies According to Abhi

My Top 3 movies of All-time

Lagaan:Once Upon a Time in India

The people of a small village in Victorian India stake their future on a game of cricket against their ruthless British rulers.

Gangs of Wasipur

A story of a Village name as wasipur. The story is based on the revenge of a son for his father.

Rang De Basanti

The story of six young Indians who assist an English woman to film a documentary on the freedom fighters from their past, and the events that lead them to relive the long-forgotten saga of freedom.

Mughal-E-Azam

A 16th century prince falls in love with a court dancer and battles with his emperor father.

The Touch

Anirudh Parmar is blind, but he does not let this impairment bind him down. He runs a school for the blind as a Principal. He meets with social worker, Kavita. Kavita is attracted to him, and he slowly also gets attracted to her. When marriage is proposed, both agree to marry each other. Then Anirudh starts having doubts whether this marriage is going to succeed, as he feels he is being patronized, and pittied.

Koshish

A speech and hearing-impaired couple persists through unforgiving obstacles to be accepted by society and live a life of dignity.

3 Idiots

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently, even as the rest of the world called them "idiots".
